  2. Deadline

    • Each match must be completed by 12:00 BD Time.
    • If the deadline is exceeded, the match will be considered invalid.

  3. Penalty for breaking the rules

    • If a player uses a different device or plays with a different ID, his match will be forfeit.
    • If someone exceeds the time limit, the opponent will be given a walkover.

  4. In case of group matches

    There will be no injuries or additional time in group matches. However, if any knockout situation is created by the admin group without any announcement, the match admin will inform about the injuries.

  6. Regarding lag, slow, disconnected

    Procedure for Lag/Slow Disconnection:

    • In case of lag/slow, the screen recorder should be turned on and the match should be disconnected by pausing the game with clear evidence.
    • However, the official/matchday admin can give a yellow card for disconnecting for â€˜any issueâ€™ other than lag/slow evidence.
    • Article 4 should be followed regarding the video rules of disconnected matches.

    Rematch Protocol:

    • For the remaining minutes of the match, the completed minutes should be subtracted from the total 93 minutes and the remaining time should be played from the beginning of the rematch. The match time must be determined and the squad/manager/formation of the game can be changed if necessary.
    • Multiple cards can be given for multiple disconnections. However, the cards should be given within 10 minutes of the disconnection and before the start of the rematch.
    • In case of creating a room in a disconnected match, it will not be considered who had the ball. The person who will show the win will have to create a room in the rematch at his/her own will.

    Goal Counting:

    • All goals clearly proven by screenshots or screen records added to the scoreboard or in the ball goal bar will be counted.
    • Sometimes, the game continues for some time even after one side is disconnected. In that case, if goal proof can be provided, the goal will be counted. But goals after 1 second of the scheduled time of the rematch will not be counted.

    Player Responsibility & Network Issues:

    • If the eFootball app crashes during the game and the game is closed, it is an internal disconnected issue. In this case, the sole responsibility lies with the player's own device. The opponent will not compromise/compensate for anything.
    • In both cases of electrical failure and wifi disconnected, [Network Error] is displayed instead of win/lose during the match. If lose/network error is shown from the lead, a video with the user ID || device must be provided. Otherwise, the lead will not count.

    Red Card/Forfeit Scenario:

    • If a player in the In-Game team receives a red card during the match and leaves the game without a slow game/lag proof video, the player will be given a red card.
